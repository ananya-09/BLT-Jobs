Company Description
OWASP BLT (Bug Logging Tool) is a project under the OWASP Foundation, focused on developing open-source tools for structured vulnerability reporting, bug tracking, and security automation. It is a community-driven initiative committed to building modular services, APIs, dashboards, applications, and automation bots for enhanced security workflows. All developments are conducted transparently under OWASP governance and distributed with open-source licensing. The project also fosters research and contributor engagement to support a secure and collaborative ecosystem.

Role Description
This is a volunteer remote role for a Content Writer at OWASP BLT. The Content Writer will create, write, and edit content related to project updates, security documentation, and community communications. The Content Writer will collaborate closely with the BLT project team to ensure all written materials align with project goals and the OWASP mission.

## Requirements

- Proficient in Web Content Writing, Technical Writing, and Creative Writing.

- Strong background in Content Strategy, Research, and Community Engagement.

- Experience with Editing and Proofreading to ensure accuracy and clarity of content.

- Familiarity with technology topics, particularly related to Vulnerability Reporting and Security.

- Effective written and verbal communication skills.

- Ability to work independently and collaborate effectively within a global, volunteer-driven team.

- Experience with open-source communities or prior contributions to OWASP projects is a plus.

- Passionate about fostering security awareness and education.
